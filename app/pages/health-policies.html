Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request menstrual leave?
Request through the HR portal or this app at least 24 hours in advance when possible. No medical documentation is required for the first 12 days per year. Select "Menstrual Leave" as the absence type.
What menopause accommodations are available?
Accommodations include flexible work hours, temperature control at workstations, access to cool-down areas, permission for additional breaks, and specialized health consultations with our health partner clinics.
Does our insurance cover fertility treatments?
Yes, our premium health plan covers up to $15,000 lifetime maximum for fertility treatments including IUI and IVF. The standard plan offers $5,000 coverage. Consult the benefits handbook for details.
How do I access mental health resources?
Access through the Employee Assistance Program (EAP) portal or call the 24/7 hotline at 1-800-555-0123. All employees receive 8 free counseling sessions per year, with additional coverage through insurance.
